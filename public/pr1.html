The best kdramas according to Minnie

My top 3 kdramas all-time

All of us are dead

This is a drama where the whole school turned into zombies. Some school students escaped from them and got into safe place.

Boys over Flowers

This is a drama of four handsome boys studying in korean top school. The rich boy who is son of his school director fell in love with the poor girl who get into the school by schoralship.

The tail of nine-tailed

This is a drama of a nine tailed fox who lived more than nine thousand years waiting for his love. He is the mountain god. We also have season 2.
